Rax Hunting is team of dedicated hunters with a mission to tag out on trophy bucks. Join the pursuit!

We take images of your trophy rack and turn that into your own customized apparel for you to wear!

Apparel Options:

  • Trucker Cap
  • Beanie
  • T-shirt
  • Sweatshirt
  • Coozie

What We Do

  1. You send us an image of your trophy.
  2. We edit the rack into a silhouette.
  3. We then put it on our trademarked Rax Hunting logo text.
  4. After that we send a proof to you.
  5. Once you aprove it, the production is on its way!
